KtM

Roles: 2D Artist & Programmer

About the Game

KtM (Kill The Mayor) is a 1st person game where players attend a party for chance to get closer to the Mayor. This will involve socializing with others and improving your status in order to get up close with the mayor for an thrilling encounter.

Mechanics

KtM features first person controls (WASD keyboard + mouse) with the ability to talk to other party goers. By completing tasks players can earn masks that will open up new conversations and futher tasks. The goal is by completing tasks and gaining masks for a chance to get close to the mayor.
