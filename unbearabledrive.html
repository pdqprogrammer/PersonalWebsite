unBearable Drive

Role: Solo Developer

About the Game

unBearable Drive was created for the ATX Game Makers 2023 Hibernation Game Jam-A-Thon. This game was built around theme of Hibernation. The concept of the game is that players control three bears trying to make it home from a night of partying to get some sweet Hi-bear-nation (not sorry).

Mechanics

unBearable Drive has players steering a car in a precarious manner since the Bears just got out of partying. Players also have the ability to slow down and speed up the car but when players hit something they lose a Bear and become a bit faster. Avoid cars, signs, and potholes as you try to reach home on the most dangerous road out there. The game is playable with Keyboard/Mouse for PC, on screen controls for Android, and controller for both!
