Decor Hero

Role: Solo Developer

About the Game

Decor Hero was created for the 2019 Global Game Jam and won an award for Highest APM for Development. This game was built around the idea of No Place Like Home. The concept of the game is that players are given a gun that can shoot furniture, paint the walls, and lay down flooring.

Mechanics

Decor Hero follows standard First Person controls, where players move around using the WASD keys, shoot with left click, and can scroll through the different furniture to shoot out. Players can shoot paint colors onto all walls and furniture. Players can shoot flooring patterns down on the ground. Player can end the game when they feel they have decorated the room enough to their liking.
